    MCQ 1
    Q1. Charles Babbage was a ___?
    • A) Mathematician
    • B) Physician
    • C) Musician
    • D) Cricketer
    Babbage was a mathematician (also a mechanical engineer historically, but that wasn't an option here). This is why he's called the "Father of Computer."
    MCQ 2
    Q2. Which of the following devices was responsible for the transition from First to Second Generation of computers?
    • A) Chip
    • B) Transistor
    • C) Vacuum Tube
    • D) Large Scale Integration
